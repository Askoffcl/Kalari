About Kalaripayattu

Kalaripayattu is one of the oldest martial arts in the world, originating in Kerala, India. More than just a fighting technique, it is a system of physical training, healing practices, and spiritual discipline.

History

The word "Kalari" means battlefield, and "Payattu" means fight or practice. With origins tracing back over 3,000 years, it combines strikes, kicks, grappling, weaponry, and healing techniques.

Philosophy

At its core, Kalaripayattu is about balance between body, mind, and soul. It teaches respect, discipline, and harmony with nature.

Modern Relevance

Today, Kalaripayattu is practiced not only as a martial art but also as a form of fitness, meditation, and performance art. It continues to inspire people around the world.
